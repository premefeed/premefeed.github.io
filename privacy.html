Terms & Conditions/Privacy Policy

Uptime

Preme Feed's goal is to maintain 100% server uptime but there may be rare occasions that our servers are down due to maintenance, updates, or other unspecified reasoning.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Preme Feed collects, uses, maintains and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the PremeFeed.github.io website ("Site"). This privacy policy applies to the Site and all products and services offered by Preme Feed.

Personal identification information

Users may be prompted to enter information such as name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and credit card information in order to use the one-click purchase feature. None of the information required to purchase merchandise will be sent or stored on any of Preme Feed's servers. All user-submitted information is locally encrypted on the user's device. You will be required to enter a 4 digit pin in order to add a credit card to Preme Feed. This feature prevents unauthorized access to your card information. If the passcode is entered wrong 10 times in a row, all app data will be cleared and your card information will no longer be present anywhere within the app or on the phone.

Compliance with children's online privacy protection act

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our Site from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.

Acceptance of these terms

By using Preme Feed for iOS & Android, along with the PremeFeed.github.io site, you are accepting this policy.
